AI Features – Multi-Tenant Healthcare CRM
1. AI Appointment Assistant
Enables patients to book, reschedule, or cancel appointments via chat (WhatsApp, web, mobile).
Understands natural language queries and automatically assigns available doctors and time slots.
Synchronizes with doctor calendars in real time.


2. AI Symptom Checker & Pre-Consult Triage
Collects patient symptoms before consultation.
Suggests appropriate department, urgency level, and preliminary tests.
Generates a structured summary for doctors prior to the visit.


3. AI Clinical Notes Automation
Converts doctor voice or text input into structured clinical notes.
Supports SOAP and EMR-ready formats.
Reduces manual documentation time and improves record accuracy.


4. AI Prescription Assistance
Suggests medicines and dosages based on symptoms and patient history.
Detects potential drug interactions and allergy conflicts.
Requires final approval from the attending doctor.


5. AI No-Show Prediction
Predicts patients likely to miss appointments using historical data.
Triggers automated reminders, rescheduling, or safe double-booking.
Improves appointment utilization and clinic revenue.


6. AI Smart Follow-Up Engine
Automatically identifies patients requiring follow-ups.
Determines optimal follow-up timing and communication channel.
Sends personalized reminders and care instructions.


7. AI Patient Churn Prediction
Identifies patients at risk of not returning.
Triggers automated engagement workflows to improve retention.


8. AI Lab Report Analysis
Analyzes lab reports and highlights abnormal values.
Provides simplified explanations for patients.
Flags critical cases for immediate doctor attention.


9. AI Revenue & Performance Analytics
Predicts monthly revenue and appointment trends.
Analyzes doctor and department performance.
Identifies operational bottlenecks and growth opportunities.
10. AI WhatsApp & Chat Support Agent
Handles patient queries related to appointments, services, pricing, and reports.
Provides 24/7 automated responses with human handover support.
Integrates seamlessly with clinic and hospital workflows.


11. AI Patient Risk Scoring (Enterprise)
Assesses health risks for chronic and high-risk patients.
Supports preventive care and early intervention strategies.


12. AI Voice-to-Text Documentation
Converts spoken consultations into structured medical records.
Improves documentation speed and accuracy.
