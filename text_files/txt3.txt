Project Features – Multi-Tenant Healthcare CRM
1 Patient Management
Create & manage patient profiles (basic info, demographics)
Store medical history, allergies, prescriptions, and lab reports
Family/dependent profile management
Patient tagging & segmentation (VIP, chronic, follow-up, insurance type)
Document uploads (scans, reports, consent forms)
Consent & compliance management (HIPAA, GDPR, local laws)
Patient portal access (view records, download prescriptions)
Tiering: Basic for clinics, advanced for hospitals/enterprise (full EMR + history timeline)
2 Appointment & Scheduling
Doctor-wise and department-wise scheduling
Online appointment booking (web & mobile)
Walk-in & token-based queue management
Automatic slot allocation based on availability
Appointment rescheduling & cancellation
Multi-branch scheduling support
SMS/WhatsApp/email appointment reminders
Tiering: Basic for small clinics, full multi-department + AI-powered scheduling for hospitals & enterprise
3 Billing & Payments
Consultation billing and multi-service invoicing
Multi-payment gateway integration (cards, UPI, wallets)
Partial & advance payments
Refund & cancellation management
Insurance claim support and pre-authorization tracking
GST / tax & compliance reports
Tiering: Basic invoicing for clinics, full insurance + reporting for hospitals/enterprise

4 Staff & Doctor Management
Staff & doctor profiles
Role-based access and permissions
Scheduling, shift management, and leave tracking
Performance & KPI tracking
Commission / revenue tracking per doctor
Tiering: Basic roles for small clinics, detailed reporting & multi-department management for hospitals/enterprise
5 Lab & Diagnostics
Lab test catalog & sample collection management
Lab result uploads & notifications
Integration with external labs
Test result history & patient report management
Tiering: Optional add-on for clinics, integrated for hospitals

6 Pharmacy & Inventory (Optional Module)
Medicine inventory tracking
Prescription-to-pharmacy automation
Stock alerts & expiry tracking
Supplier management
Billing integration
Tiering: Optional for small clinics, standard for hospitals
7 Communication & Engagement
Two-way messaging (WhatsApp, SMS, email)
Broadcast messages (health alerts, offers, campaigns)
Patient feedback & rating system
Push notifications via portal/app
Tiering: Basic reminders for clinics, automated campaigns for hospitals/enterprise
8 AI-Powered Features
Auto clinical note generation from doctor input
Symptom checker & pre-consult triage
Predictive follow-ups & no-show detection
Personalized patient engagement & reminders
Voice-to-text support for notes


Tiering: Optional for small clinics, standard for hospitals/enterprise
9 Reporting & Analytics
Dashboard for revenue, appointments, and doctor performance
Department-wise & doctor-wise analytics
Patient retention, follow-ups, and no-show reports
Custom report builder
AI-powered insights (trends, risk predictions)
Tiering: Basic summary for clinics, detailed + AI insights for hospitals/enterprise
10 Security & Compliance
Multi-tenant data isolation
Data encryption (at rest & in transit)
Role-based access control & audit logs
HIPAA/GDPR/compliance for local regulations
Multi-factor authentication
Tiering: Core for all tenants
11 Admin & Configuration
Tenant onboarding & management
Feature toggling (enable/disable modules per tenant)
Custom branding (logo, theme) per tenant
Multi-language & multi-currency support
API integrations (payment, accounting, external systems)


Tiering: Basic branding for clinics, full customization for hospitals/enterprise
