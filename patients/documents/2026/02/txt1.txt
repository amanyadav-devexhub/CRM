Project Description:
The Multi-Tenant Healthcare CRM is a cloud-based, subscription-driven software platform designed to streamline operations for clinics, hospitals, and healthcare providers of any size. The platform centralizes patient management, appointment scheduling, billing, lab integration, staff management, and communication, providing an AI-powered, automated, and secure system to improve patient care and clinic efficiency.
Key highlights of the platform:
Multi-tenant architecture to serve clinics, hospitals, and healthcare chains in one system.
Modular and subscription-based pricing to suit small clinics, medium hospitals, and enterprise healthcare organizations.
AI-powered automation for patient follow-ups, clinical notes, triage, and predictive insights.
Communication-first approach with WhatsApp, SMS, and email integration for patient engagement.
Secure, compliant, and scalable architecture supporting HIPAA/GDPR and other local regulations.
This system allows healthcare providers to reduce administrative workload, improve patient satisfaction, enhance revenue management, and make data-driven decisions.
Project Scope:
1. Patient Management
Maintain patient profiles with demographics, medical history, allergies, and documents.
Family / dependent profiles.
Consent and compliance management.
Patient tagging and segmentation.
2. Appointment & Scheduling
Doctor-wise and department-wise calendars.
Online booking, walk-ins, and token management.
Automated slot allocation and reminders.
Queue management and multi-branch support.


3. Billing & Payments
Tenant-specific billing rules.
Multi-payment gateway support.
Insurance claim management and policy tracking.
Invoicing, receipts, taxes, and refunds.
Optional subscription add-ons for pharmacy and lab billing.
4. Staff & Doctor Management
Role-based access and permissions.
Scheduling, shift planning, and leave management.
Performance tracking and reporting.
5. Lab & Diagnostics Management
Lab test catalog and sample collection tracking.
Lab result uploads and notifications.
Integration with external laboratories.


6. AI-Powered Features
Auto clinical note generation.
Symptom checker & pre-consult triage.
Predictive follow-up reminders and no-show detection.
Personalized patient engagement recommendations.


7. Communication & Engagement
Two-way messaging via WhatsApp, SMS, and email.
Automated reminders, notifications, and campaigns.
Patient portal and mobile app access.
Feedback and rating system.


8. Reporting & Analytics
Daily, monthly, and yearly dashboards for revenue, appointments, and doctor performance.
AI-powered insights per department, doctor, and branch.
Custom report generation.


9. Security & Compliance
Data isolation for multi-tenancy.
Encryption at rest and in transit.
Role-based access control and audit logs.
HIPAA/GDPR/local regulation compliance.


10. Admin & Configuration
Tenant onboarding and management.
Feature toggling per tenant (modular system).
Multi-language and multi-currency support.
API integrations with payments, accounting, and external systems.


Target Users / Tenants
Small clinics (1–5 doctors)
Medium hospitals and multi-specialty clinics (5–50 doctors)
Large hospitals and healthcare chains (50+ doctors, multi-branch)


Project Goals
Provide a centralized, multi-tenant SaaS platform for healthcare operations.
Automate routine tasks with AI-powered tools to reduce workload.
Offer tiered subscription plans to serve clinics, hospitals, and enterprises.
Ensure data security, compliance, and scalability.
Improve patient engagement, retention, and satisfaction.
